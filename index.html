About us

The studios goals, history and staff roles

Our studio has a small team and that's what makes us special. Our mission is to bring fun back to roblox again, without caring about money. Tarik, our Lead developer and founder has founded this studio in 2025 to bring fun back to roblox. Dino, our co-founder and community manager, cares about the community and makes sure everyone is happy. And lastly, Dani who is the scripter/ui designer, website designer and bot developer.


Projects

We're currently working on 2 projects, Research and LOST_FILE:\

The first project is called Research, a story game where there is 2 paths good and bad doctor. You cure diseases or make new species like a new animal or even build your own business, but at one point you will have to choose a path, good or bad doctor. When you cure a disease you get good honor and bad honor when you make a disease and when you cure a disease you get a fragment of it. The second project is a horror game called LOST_FILE:\, and in it you get trapped in an unfinished game and you have to finish an NPC for him to help you escape. But he wont be as you expect.
